How We Work

Our End-to-End Process

We manage every step, from the farm to your port, ensuring quality and transparency.

1

Sourcing & QC

We partner directly with farmer co-ops and perform rigorous inbound inspections for moisture, grade, and purity at our facility.

2

Processing & Packing

Our facility handles sorting, grading, and packing into export-ready 50kg bags (or custom) to preserve product integrity.
