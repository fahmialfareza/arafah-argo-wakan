Our Quality Control Process

We don't just trade; we ensure quality. Every batch undergoes a rigorous 4-step inspection process before it leaves our facility.

1

Inbound Inspection

Raw materials from farmers are checked for moisture content and physical defects upon arrival.

2

Sorting & Grading

Products are meticulously sorted by size, color, and quality grade (e.g., Coffee Grade 1 vs 4B) to meet specific orders.

3

Final Lab Test

Random samples are tested (internally or via 3rd party like SGS) for chemical standards and purity.

4

Pre-Shipment Check

Final visual inspection during container stuffing to ensure secure loading and correct packaging.
